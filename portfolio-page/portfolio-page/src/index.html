Bike Safety

This will focus on how to stay safe when riding a bike. For example, knowing the rules of traffic riding a bicycle and making good decisons while riding a bike. Riding and turning slower during rainy days, signaling to other cars for turning, and obeying traffic laws are all examples of good bike saftey.

Biking Gear

This section will focus on what a person needs when comutting on a bike. These accesories will make one's ride a lot more convienient and be there in case of an emergency. For example, a backpack full of equipment such as a multitool in case of an emergency repair, a spare inner tube, a portable pump are all gear that a bicyclist should bring with when commuting. Having this gear handy will help one be prepared for their journey if a problem arises.
